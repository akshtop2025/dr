Dr. Imran Patel is a highly experienced pediatrician and a recognized authority in neonatology.
With over a decade of experience in private practice, he also serves as an Assistant Professor at a prestigious
government hospital. Dr. Patel’s expertise extends beyond traditional medical practice;
he is a skilled wordsmith, often crafting shayaris, and enjoys sculpting fitness routines in his spare time.

Dedicated to the holistic well-being of infants and children, Dr. Patel’s commitment to healthcare
goes beyond the confines of his medical practice. He is deeply involved in charitable healthcare initiatives,
striving to raise awareness of health issues among underprivileged communities. As a proud Indian,
Dr. Patel is passionate about contributing to societal upliftment through his work.
